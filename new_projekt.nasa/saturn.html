Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and is known for its prominent ring system. It is a gas giant with an atmosphere primarily composed of hydrogen and helium.

The planet has a striking appearance due to its pale yellow hue, which comes from ammonia clouds in its upper atmosphere.

Saturn has 82 moons, with Titan being the largest and one of the most interesting bodies in the solar system due to its dense atmosphere and liquid methane lakes.

Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun in our Solar System. It is known for its unique blue-green color, which is due to the methane in its atmosphere.

Uranus has a diameter of about 50,724 kilometers, making it the third largest planet in terms of diameter. It has a faint ring system and 27 known moons.

The planet has a rotational tilt of 98 degrees, making it appear to rotate on its side compared to other planets.

Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is often referred to as the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance caused by iron oxide on its surface.

The planet has the largest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons, which is about 13.6 miles (22 km) high. Mars also has a thin atmosphere, composed mostly of carbon dioxide.

Evidence suggests that liquid water once flowed on Mars, and there are ongoing missions to explore the planet for signs of past life.
